About Us

Ebri-Climate Actions and Computers is a multidisciplinary organization committed to solving climate challenges through environmental projects, renewable energy solutions, capacity training and technology services.

Founded by Ebri, Joshua I., our mission is to empower communities, youth and institutions with the tools and knowledge to create sustainable development.

Our Services

Climate & Environmental Projects

Tree planting, waste management, environmental restoration, community outreach.

Renewable Energy (Solar)

Solar installation, training, maintenance and energy consultancy services.

Capacity Training

Digital skills, climate education, leadership and entrepreneurship development.

Computer & Technology Services

IT services, computer training, data solutions and tech support.

Projects & Impact

We have implemented environmental education programs, tree planting campaigns, digital training initiatives and clean energy projects across communities in Nigeria.
